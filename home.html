  The early explorers and adventurers who came to North America had a profound impact upon the culture of modern America, shaping the following centuries in many ways. From the first voyage of Columbus in 1492, to the French fur traders and the English Puritans settling in America, all of these cultures had an impact of the American life today. Many of their lives revolved around their desire for God, Glory, and Gold, and through this, they shaped American society.
